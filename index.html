    About

    Ledger is a small, fast expense tracker for students. Log what you spend, watch it against a cap you set, and find anything with regular-expression search. Your data stays in your browser — nothing is uploaded.

    Built with semantic HTML, mobile-first CSS, and modular vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks.
